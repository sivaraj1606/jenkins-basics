JENKINS CRON CHEAT SHEET
1. Cron Format (Always remember this first)
MIN HOUR DAY MONTH DAY_OF_WEEK
Example:
H/2 * * * *
 
2. Symbols (Core understanding)
Symbol	Meaning
*	Every value
*/n	Every n units
H	Hash (load balancing by Jenkins)
,	Multiple values
-	Range
 
3. Basic Schedules
* * * * *
→ Every minute
*/5 * * * *
→ Every 5 minutes
*/10 * * * *
→ Every 10 minutes
0 * * * *
→ Every hour (at minute 0)
 
4. Jenkins Preferred (IMPORTANT)
H * * * *
→ Every hour (distributed)
H/2 * * * *
→ Every 2 minutes (distributed)
H/15 * * * *
→ Every 15 minutes
👉 Key point:
Use H instead of * in Jenkins to avoid load spikes
 
5. Daily Schedules
H 0 * * *
→ Daily at midnight
H 2 * * *
→ Daily at 2 AM
30 14 * * *
→ Daily at 2:30 PM
 
6. Weekly Schedules
H * * * 0
→ Every Sunday
H 9 * * 1-5
→ Weekdays at 9 AM
H 18 * * 6
→ Every Saturday evening
 
7. Monthly Schedules
H 0 1 * *
→ 1st day of every month
H 12 15 * *
→ 15th of every month at noon
 
8. Multiple Values
0 9,18 * * *
→ 9 AM and 6 PM daily
H 9-17 * * 1-5
→ Every hour from 9–5 on weekdays
